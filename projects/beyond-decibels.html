Beyond Decibels

Madison, Wisconsin · Soundscape policy · GIS analysis

Beyond Decibels argues for a soundscape-based approach to urban noise policy—moving past single-number decibel thresholds to consider lived experience, equity, and place.

This project combines cartographic storytelling with spatial analysis to explore how noise exposure and vulnerability intersect across Madison.

Methods / tools: GIS mapping, spatial overlays, policy framing, and narrative design.
